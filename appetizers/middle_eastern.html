Middle Eastern cuisine is celebrated for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and wholesome ingredients.Here are some delicious Middle Eastern appetizers that you can try at home:

How to Make Hummus:

Ingredients for Hummus:

  • 1 can (400g) of chickpeas (drained, reserve some liquid)
  • 2-3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Paprika and chopped parsley (for garnish)
  • Extra olive oil for drizzling

Prepare process of Hummus:

  • In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, and salt.
  • Blend until smooth. If the mixture is too thick, add some reserved chickpea liquid or water to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

Serve:

  • Transfer the hummus to a shallow bowl.
  • Use the back of a spoon to create a small swirl or well in the center.
  • Drizzle olive oil over the top, sprinkle with paprika, and garnish with chopped parsley.
  • Serve with warm pita bread, crackers, or fresh vegetable sticks.

How to Make Falafel:

Ingredients for Falafel:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight and drained)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Preparation Progress the Falafel:

  • Place the soaked chickpeas, parsley, cilantro, onion, garlic, cumin, coriander, baking powder, salt, and pepper into a food processor.
  • Blend until the mixture is coarse but holds together when pressed. Avoid over-blending into a paste.
  • Refrigerate the mixture for about 30 minutes to firm up.
  • Form the mixture into small balls or patties.

Fry the Falafel:

  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or fryer to 350Â°F (180Â°C).
  • Carefully drop the falafel balls into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the falafel with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.

Serve:

  • Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
  • Serve with tahini sauce, hummus, or stuffed in pita bread with lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.! ðŸŒ¸

How to Make Baba Ghanoush

Ingredients for Baba Ghanoush:

  • 3 medium eggplants
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika, Olive oil and chopped parsley (for garnish)

Preparation Process:

  • Preheat the oven to 200Â°C (400Â°F).
  • Prick the eggplants with a fork and place them on a baking sheet.
  • Roast for 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally, until the skin is charred and the eggplants are soft.
  • Let the eggplants cool, then peel off the charred skin.
  • In a bowl, mash the eggplant pulp with a fork or blend in a food processor.
  • Mix in the tahini, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until smooth.

Serve:

  • Transfer the baba ghanoush to a serving dish.
  • Drizzle olive oil on top and sprinkle with paprika and parsley.
  • Serve with pita bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.
