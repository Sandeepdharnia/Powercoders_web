Middle Eastern Main Courses

Middle Eastern cuisine is celebrated for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and wholesome ingredients. Here are some delicious Middle Eastern Main Courses that you can try at home:

How to Make Lamb Kofta with Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients Lamb Kofta:

  • 500g ground lamb
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Preparation of the Lamb Kofta:

  • Mix lamb, onion, garlic, spices, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  • Shape into oval-shaped patties around skewers.
  • Grill or pan-fry until browned and cooked through.

How to Make Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients Chicken Shawarma:

  • 500g chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation of the Chicken Shawarma:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper for at least 2 hours.
  • Cook on a grill or in a hot skillet until browned.
