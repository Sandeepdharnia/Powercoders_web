How to Make Jollof Rice (West Africa)

Ingredients for Jollof Rice:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
