How to Make Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients for Chocolate Lava Cake:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (plus extra for greasing)
  • 6 oz (170g) bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Powdered sugar (for garnish)

Preparation of Chocolate Lava Cake:

  • Preheat your oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C).
  • Grease four ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  • In a double boiler or microwave, melt the butter and chocolate until smooth.
  • In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract until frothy.
  • Gently fold the melted chocolate into the egg mixture.
  • Sift the flour and salt into the batter, then fold gently until fully combined.
  • Divide the mixture evenly among the ramekins.
  • Bake for 12-14 minutes or until the edges are set, but the center remains soft.

Serve:

  • Let the cakes cool for 2 minutes, then invert onto a plate.
  • Dust with powdered sugar and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or fresh berries! ðŸŒ¸

How to Make Baklava

Ingredients Baklava:

  • 1 package (16 oz) phyllo dough
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups chopped nuts (pistachios, walnuts, or almonds)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation of the Baklava:

  • Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C).
  • In a bowl, mix chopped nuts, sugar, and cinnamon.
  • Grease a baking dish and layer 10 sheets of phyllo dough, brushing melted butter between each sheet.
  • Spread a thin layer of the nut mixture on top.
  • Repeat the layers, ending with 10 sheets of phyllo dough on top.
  • Cut into diamond or square shapes using a sharp knife.
  • Bake for 40-45 minutes or until golden brown.
  • In a saucepan, combine honey, water, lemon juice, and vanilla. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Pour the hot syrup over the baked baklava immediately after it comes out of the oven.

Serve:

  • Allow the baklava to cool completely and absorb the syrup before serving. Serve with hot tea or coffee! ðŸŒ¸

How to Make Tiramisu

Ingredients Tiramisu:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 3 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 8 oz (225g) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped to soft peaks
  • 1 package (7 oz) ladyfinger biscuits
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)

Preparation of the Tiramisu:

  • Combine the coffee and liqueur (if using) in a shallow bowl.
  • In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
  • Gently fold the mascarpone into the egg yolk mixture.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  • Dip each ladyfinger briefly into the coffee mixture and layer them in a dish.
  • Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  • Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

Serve:

  • Dust the top with cocoa powder before serving. Serve chilled in slices or scoops! ðŸŒ¸

How to Make Pavlova

Ingredients Pavlova:

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • 2 cups fresh fruit (berries, kiwi, mango)

Preparation of the Pavlova:

  • Preheat your oven to 250Â°F (120Â°C).
  • In a mixing bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form.
  • Gradually add sugar, one tablespoon at a time, until stiff, glossy peaks form.
  • Gently fold in vinegar, cornstarch, and vanilla.
  • Spread the mixture into a circular shape on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  • Bake for 1 hour, then turn off the oven and let the meringue cool completely inside.

Serve:

  • Top the cooled meringue with whipped cream and arrange fresh fruit on top. Slice and serve immediately! ðŸŒ¸
